### 5.3 KfW 455-B
- **Guidebook likely presents as active programme**  
- **Compendium confirms:** SUSPENDED since January 2025; restart Spring 2026  
- **Action:** Add note to Part VIII Germany grants section.

### 5.4 Quebec PAD Option 1
- **Guidebook likely presents as active**  
- **Compendium confirms:** SUSPENDED since April 2025  
- **Action:** Add note to Part VIII Canada grants section.

### 5.5 AOTA built environment standard gap
- **Compendium states:** "AOTA has not published a standalone built environment design standard"  
- **Batch 2 found:** AOTA 2022 Position Statement on Inclusive Environments (AJOT 76 Supplement 3)  
- **Action:** Update compendium record for A2 (US) — the position statement does not fully close the gap (it is not a design standard) but is the closest current AOTA publication. Update compendium note to: "AOTA has not published a standalone built environment design standard; the 2022 Position Statement on Inclusive Environments (AJOT 76 Supplement 3) defines OT's role in inclusive environments but does not specify dimensional or specification criteria."

### 5.6 DeafSpace evidence classification

- **Action:** The Marjaba (2024) paper is already referenced in the compendium. Add the DEAF elevators paper (Buildings 14(10):3300) as new evidence; note the compendium's "no POEs" finding remains accurate for DeafSpace itself.
