# Evidence landscape — A-18 (RT60)

*The transparency artifact (S1, owner-reaffirmed): make available **all jurisdictions / international
standards / precedents**; **admit extrapolation** when venturing a suggestion; **admit a thin base**. Every
row carries a provenance-strength: `direct` · `inadequate` · `extrapolated-from(X)` · `absent`.*

## Made available — what the corpus holds

**Direct primary evidence (● Tier-1, 16 studies):** classroom reverberation / speech-perception research in
hearing-impaired and typical-hearing children — Iglehart (REF-00325, 00578, 00588), Neuman (00577),
Wroblewski (00576), Prodi (00581), Spratford (00582), Wu (00586), plus Marzi 2024/25 on neurodivergent
indoor comfort (00726/00727). Jurisdictions: US ×11, INT, UK, IN, IT. `direct`.

**International standards / codes (11, across 13 jurisdictions) — the code-floor (◐, T4–6):**
ANSI/ASA S12.60 (US, T4), PAS 6463 (UK, T4), BB93 (UK, T5), DIN 18041 (DE, T5), NS 8175 (NO, T5),
UNI 11532-2 (IT, T5), AS/NZS 2107 (AU/NZ, T5), AIJES-S001 (JP, T5), SBi-anvisning 218 (DA, T5),
GB 50118 (CN, T6). **Made available in full** — but see *inadequate* below: their numeric thresholds are
un-migrated, and the non-English ones are held via English metadata.

**Precedents (built exemplars demonstrating a value achieved):** **`absent`** in the corpus for this item —
no linked case-study/POE showing an RT60 target achieved in a real inclusive building. A named search
target (Nordic/Japanese school-acoustics exemplars are the likely home).

## Admitted — where we are extrapolating (labeled, as *suggestions* not determinations)

- **Person-mode value = `extrapolated-from(population range + items.pmp_delta_min)`.** There is no Tier-1
  person-level study; the suggestion "OT tightens the population target by up to 0.05 s for greater
  individual need" is extrapolated from the population evidence plus the item's recorded PMP delta. Offered
  as an approach to consider (S2), not a graded determination.
- **Universal 0.55 s applied as an all-occupants floor** is `direct` from typical-hearing T1 — but its use
  as a *design-for-all* floor across mixed populations carries a small extrapolation (typical-hearing
  sample → general population); labeled `extrapolated-from(typical-hearing T1)` where it stands in for
  "everyone."

## Why this matters

Nothing above is hidden to make A-18 look more finished than it is. The **value** is strong and `direct`
where it counts (HI-children 0.3 s, ● T1); the **gaps** — person cell, Co-1, precedents, code-floor values,
non-English content — are named with their provenance-strength so the reader sees exactly how far the
evidence reaches and where we are only venturing a suggestion.
