**governance/decision-protocol.md** (CANONICAL, NEW): 8 sections + change log; 22K bytes / 356 lines.

§1 names **five decision categories**: D-DOCT (doctrinal — mission, CRPD posture, taxonomy, evidence-tier hierarchy); D-METH (methodological — evidence-state machine, convergence rules, freshness windows); D-SCHEMA (schema/data-structure — Pydantic entities, enum values, format patterns); D-OP (operational — branch protection, CI matrix, PAT scope); D-PRES (presentation — voice patterns, plain-language register). Categories are mutually exclusive at the level of primary effect; cross-category effect cross-references the secondary in §3 fields.

§2 names **three delegation categories**: DG-NON (non-delegable — project owner alone), DG-REVIEW (agent decides; project owner reviews), DG-AUTO (agent decides; logged; surfaces only on downstream inconsistency). §2.2 default-delegation table maps category to default. §2.4 names ten always-DG-NON decision types: mission/CRPD posture; population taxonomy; evidence-tier definitions; jurisdiction inclusion; major work-products; Co-1 corpus; licensing; trajectory; misuse-vector retirement; counsel-queue resolution.

§3 specifies the **capture protocol**: 16 required fields per record (decision_id, category, delegation, delegation_rationale, summary, outcome, rationale, alternatives_considered, decision_date, decided_by, model_routing, effort_level, decision_artifacts, predecessors, supersedes, status, review_status, notes). §3.3 rationale-length norms per category (D-DOCT 3–10 sentences; D-METH 2–5; D-SCHEMA 1–3; D-OP 1; D-PRES 1–2). §3.4 names three rationale anti-patterns (tautology, authority-without-content, outcome-as-rationale).

§4 specifies the **standardised model-routing notation** that resolves L-05: `{model_tier}/{effort_level}/{reasoning_modifier}` where tier ∈ {opus, sonnet, haiku, human, legacy}, effort ∈ {150, 125, 100, 75, 50, none}, modifier ∈ {synth, arbitrate, extract, format, route, none}. Regex enforced. Old notation forms ("Opus session", "best-practice synthesis", etc.) are deprecated; surfacing them in new content is a validator error. Phase tables and skill index update to canonical notation prospectively (sweep of existing files is a separate task).

§5 specifies the **decision register** at `data/decisions/decision_register.yaml`. Per-decision detail at `data/decisions/D-NNNN.yaml` optional.
