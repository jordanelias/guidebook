## Key Data Points Extracted

**Stark et al. 2021 (JAMA Network Open):** US RCT, n=310 community-dwelling older adults at high fall risk. Home hazard removal programme. doi: 10.1001/jamanetworkopen.2021.22044. Significant — directly tests the targeted intervention model that OTIS (untargeted) failed to show benefit for.

**Dalvand et al. 2024:** Iran RCT (n=44) — home modifications reduce falls in older adults with **dementia**. First dementia-specific home modification RCT found. Connects health-outcomes evidence to the dementia FDR domain.

**Jutkowitz & Gitlin 2012:** Cost-effectiveness analysis of the Gitlin multicomponent intervention. Provides ICER data for home modification programmes — directly relevant to Part 11 §11.3.5 economic evaluation methods.
