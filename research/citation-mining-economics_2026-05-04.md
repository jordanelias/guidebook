### Escalation flags — Session 2

🟡 **Szanton citation correction** — CAPABLE Medicaid savings paper is Szanton et al. 2018 (JAGS), not 2017 as currently cited in project files. DOI: 10.1111/jgs.15143. Economics.json and throughline-health-outcomes.md need citation correction.

🟡 **CAPABLE updated economics** — $20,000/person Medicare savings continuing for 2 years post-intervention (2025 data). Programme cost $3,000/person (Baltimore) or $1,300 cap for home mods (St. Louis). Now in 34 US states. BCR confirmed at ~10:1 combined Medicare/Medicaid.

🟡 **Tsuchiya-Ito chain — HIGH YIELD** — Japanese evidence base is much deeper than project currently reflects. Five papers from same research group form a complete chain: costs by functional limitation (2022) → frailty prevention (2023) → LTCF admission reduction (2024) → 3-year expenditure analysis (2025). This is the most comprehensive non-Western housing adaptation evidence chain in the literature.

🟡 **Tsuchiya-Ito 2019** — Japan-Sweden cross-cultural comparison of housing challenges (with Iwarsson and Slaug from Lund). Connects the Japanese evidence to the Scandinavian evidence base. Not yet in project.
