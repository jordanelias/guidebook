**1. Siegelaar et al. 2025 — DEM meta-review**
- Full title: The Physical Environment and the Quality of Life and Behavior in People With Dementia: A Systematic Meta-Review
- Journal: (SAGE, doi:10.1177/30495334251345092)
- Published: June 2025
- Significance: Meta-review of reviews — highest level of evidence synthesis for DEM built environment. Reviews existing systematic reviews with evidence strength assessment. Potentially supersedes both Bowes 2019 and Marquardt 2009 as primary DEM evidence synthesis.

- Journal: Building and Environment (ScienceDirect)
- Published: January 2026
- Significance: Updated scoping review focusing specifically on sensory stimuli. May supersede or update Tola et al. 2021 for NDV sensory specifications.

**3. Cha 2025 — home modifications aging in place**
- Full title: A Systematic Review of Home Modifications for Aging in Place in Older Adults
- Journal: Healthcare (doi:10.3390/healthcare13070752)
- Published: March 2025
- 20 studies, 65% confirmed effectiveness for falls prevention, functional independence, cost savings
- Broader scope than Sheth & Cogle 2023 (includes QoL, caregiving, technology)
- Action: Add to MOB/ALL BPC. Does not supersede Clemson 2023 Cochrane (which remains the definitive falls meta-analysis).
