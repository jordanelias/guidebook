**Lifecycle cost argument (synthesis):** A grab rail costing $100–$350 can prevent a fall that results in hospitalisation costing $10,000–$50,000+. A level-access shower ($8,500–$20,000) can defer residential care ($39,520/year UK). The lifecycle ROI of accessibility features is overwhelmingly positive for features that prevent falls or defer institutional care. [Peer-reviewed lifecycle cost study not found — flagged as P1 gap]
