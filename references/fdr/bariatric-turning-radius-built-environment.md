**Clinical reasoning (wheelbase geometry):** Bariatric WCs are not simply wider standard chairs — they also have longer wheelbases and greater turning radii. The critical design implication is that the *standard 1524mm turning circle*, which is calibrated for a standard manual wheelchair, is inadequate for even mid-range bariatric power chairs. The Alltrack HD achieves 520mm turning radius at the low end; full-size bariatric chairs can require 800–890mm — translating to a minimum turning diameter of 1600–1780mm. Design must specify 1800×1800mm or larger to accommodate the realistic bariatric WC population.
