## Summary

Eliminate IntD (Intellectual Disability) as a standalone main-taxonomy population code. IntD becomes a **proxy** — its substantive provisions are redistributed as editorial notes within DEM and NDV sections. No standalone section, matrix column, or population code entry for IntD.

## Rationale

IntD evidence base is PLACEHOLDER (GAP-CR-11). All IntD provisions in the guidebook are already framed as DEM/NDV proxies. Maintaining a standalone category with no independent evidence base misrepresents the state of knowledge. Proxy notes within DEM/NDV accurately reflect the evidentiary relationship.
