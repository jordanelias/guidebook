## Summary

This Change Order implements a comprehensive structural renaming and renumbering of the Guidebook. Volumes are renamed to use Roman numerals (I, II, III). All parts are renumbered sequentially across the whole guidebook (Parts 1‚Äì13), not restarting per volume. Sections and subsections adopt ¬ß[part].[section].[subsection] format throughout. Part IV (Conflicting Requirements, Vol I stub) is deleted. Category J (Bariatric Provisions) and all BAR mentions in Volume II are deleted. The Biophilic Appendix (BIO items) and Thermal Comfort items (TC items) are moved from Part 8 to standalone Appendices B and C. Appendices are repositioned as standalone after Volume III, not within it. The Supplementary Volume is renumbered from Section I‚ÄìV to Supp. Part 1‚Äì4 with ¬ß1.x‚Äì¬ß4.x coding; Section V (Guidebook Revision Notes) is deleted entirely.
