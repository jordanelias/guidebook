# Bottom-Up Construction Cost Data — Accessible Built Environment Items
<!-- Created: 2026-04-09 18:02 | Sources: Angi, HomeAdvisor, Fixr, Lifeway Mobility, HomGuide, Remodel Inspo, specialist suppliers | Currency: USD unless noted | Data vintage: 2025–2026 -->

**Purpose:** Granular per-item and per-system cost benchmarks for disability-related residential modifications. Intended to inform Part 10 (Adaptable Readiness), economic framing in Part 1, and specification cost-awareness notes throughout Volumes I–II.

**Limitations:** All figures are US market, installed (labour + materials) unless stated otherwise. Costs vary ±40% by region. Not formal quantity surveying — indicative ranges from consumer-facing cost databases and specialist suppliers.

## Aggregate Benchmarks

| Scope | Cost Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Minor accessibility retrofit (grab bars, lever hardware, non-slip, raised seat) | $350–$1,000 | Per room; minimal disruption |
