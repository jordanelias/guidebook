### US Non-Ratification Note
The United States signed the CRPD in 2009 but the Senate has not ratified it. The ADA (1990) provides domestic disability rights protection. However, US non-ratification means it is not subject to CRPD Committee review and has no binding obligation under Article 9. This is significant for the guidebook because the US has the most developed accessibility code (ADA/A117.1) but no international accountability mechanism.
