## Key Findings (Interim — not for synthesis until LOG)

**Finding 1 (convergent, all languages searched):** SCI and MS populations have clinically documented thermoregulatory impairment. SCI (particularly tetraplegia): impaired sweating, reduced skin vasodilation, inability to perceive thermal strain. MS: Uhthoff phenomenon — even 0.5°C core temperature rise causes pseudoexacerbation. Evidence base: Tiers 1–3, well-established. Jurisdictions: UK/CA/AU/CH/ES/US.

**Finding 2 (CRITICAL GAP):** Zero Tier 1–4 sources exist that specify indoor thermal design parameters for disability populations. The clinical evidence (what happens physiologically) has not been translated into building design guidance anywhere in the retrieved literature across any jurisdiction.

**Finding 3 (Global South specific):** Tropical/subtropical contexts fundamentally change the problem: (a) SCI/MS populations in these climates face ambient temperatures chronically above the threshold that triggers thermoregulatory failure in SCI (>31°C outdoor minimum risk temperature in LMIC cities per WHO HHGL); (b) passive cooling (cross-ventilation, shading, materials) is the primary available strategy as AC is unaffordable; (c) disability + heat = compounded vulnerability — no study addresses this intersection specifically in a Global South context.

**Finding 4 (WHO Tier 4):** WHO HHGL 2018 explicitly identifies disabled persons as requiring indoor temperatures below the maximum acceptable level. Sets 18–24°C as the general healthy sedentary range. Acknowledges minimal risk temperature varies by climate (up to 31°C in LMIC cities). Does NOT provide disability-specific thermal thresholds or passive cooling design specifications.

**Finding 5 (jurisdiction gap confirmed):** No national accessible building standard in any of the 46 jurisdictions (confirmed for US, UK, CA, AU, DE, CH, ES, IN, ZA, NO, SE, EU) contains thermal performance provisions specifically for disability populations. Thermal standards exist (ASHRAE 55, EN 15251, NBC India) but are population-agnostic and calibrated for able-bodied sedentary occupants.
