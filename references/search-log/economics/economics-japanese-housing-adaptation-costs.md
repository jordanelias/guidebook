- **Key findings:**
  1. Japanese LTC insurance housing adaptation: median cost USD $1,287; max ¥200,000 lifetime (Tier 1)
  2. Housing adaptations at max cost → 28% lower LTCF admission rate (Tier 1, JAMDA 2024)
  3. Only 8.8% of JP households have "high level" barrier-free (comparable to UK 9-12%)
  4. Serviced elderly residence subsidies + tax benefits + JHF loans (DLA Piper 2023)
  5. Japan-Sweden comparison: JP housing stock younger but less accessible; adaptations less frequent and more complex
