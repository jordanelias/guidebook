  2. German cost discrepancy resolved: scope-dependent (0.35-1.26% basic vs 5-10% R-marked wheelchair)

  6. Hedonic gap confirmed: no peer-reviewed hedonic regression on disability-accessibility features in residential transactions across any jurisdiction
  7. Canadian institutional landscape mapped: CSA B652:23, CAN/ASC-2.8:2025, 2024 OBC, CMHC Housing Design Catalogue, MLI Select, provincial programmes
