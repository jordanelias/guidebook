**Evidence tier:** Tier 1 (Douglas 2024, Ainsworth 2022, Malmgren Fänge 2019)
**Filed:** 2026-04-09
**Applied:** —

**Connection:** "Quality of support provided in the home is a greater determinant of tenant quality of life than the built environment" (Douglas et al. 2024, Tier 1). **Action required:** Part 1: foundational principle — built environment is necessary but not sufficient. Part 5: multicomponent intervention model (CAPABLE structure). Cross-reference CON-0151 (OT at RFO).

**Disposition notes:** — Most important integrity disclosure in the guidebook.
