## International Housing Adaptation Grant Programmes (added 2026-05-03)

### Japan
| Programme | Max funding | Coverage | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| LTC Insurance housing adaptation | ¥200,000 lifetime (~USD $1,430) | 90% covered (10-20% copay) | 6 adaptation types; care certification required. Tsuchiya-Ito 2022: median cost $1,287. Tsuchiya-Ito 2024: 28% LTCF admission reduction at max cost. |
| Flat 35S barrier-free | 0.25% rate reduction × 10 years | Financing benefit | JHF long-term fixed-rate mortgage with accessibility provisions |
| Serviced elderly residence subsidies | Government subsidies (varies) | New construction + renovation | Tax benefits (fixed asset + real estate transfer) + JHF loans. Must be barrier-free. |

### United Kingdom
| Programme | Max funding | Coverage | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| Disabled Facilities Grant (DFG) | £30,000 max (means-tested) | Mandatory duty on local authorities | Funding trajectory: £56M (1997/98) → £185M (2013/14) → £625M (2024/25). 185% increase over 10 years. Source: Foundations UK. |
| M4(2) mandate (Dec 2025) | N/A (regulatory) | 40% national minimum new homes | NPPF reform. Previous position: voluntary, set by local plans. London Plan: 90% M4(2) + 10% M4(3). |

### New Zealand
| Programme | Max funding | Coverage | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| ACC home modification | Varies | Injury insurer covers modifications | NZ ACC: NZD $1.4B/yr in slip/trip/fall harm. Keall RCT: NZD $500 package → 9:1 BCR. |
| Bostadsanpassningsbidrag (SE) | No individual cap | Municipal responsibility | Swedish housing adaptation grant. OT assessment required. Fänge & Iwarsson 2005. |

### United States
| Programme | Max funding | Coverage | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|
| CAPABLE (Medicaid) | ~$1,300 home modifications | OT+RN+handyman, 5 months | Adopted 40+ cities. ~10:1 Medicaid savings (Szanton 2017). |
| Senior Accessible Housing Act (proposed) | $30,000 tax credit | Introduced multiple times, not passed | Congressional proposal for income tax credit covering home modifications. |
| VA CAPABLE-VA | Varies | Veterans Affairs adaptation | Implementation hybrid trial. Critical home repairs included. |
