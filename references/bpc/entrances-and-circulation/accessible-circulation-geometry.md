### Best-practice synthesis

**Lift car dimensions:**
- Minimum compliant (EN 81-70 Type 2): 1100mm wide × 1400mm deep, door on short end. This is the international baseline (UK, SE, FI, NO, DE, FR, AU, SG, CA, US proxy).
- Best practice: 1100mm × 1800mm (stretcher-capable, large power wheelchair); BS 8300 recommends 1100mm × 2000mm for new buildings.
- Power wheelchair users require 1400mm × 1600mm minimum for independent use (IDeA Center Steinfeld et al. 2010 — from MOB BPC).
- Landing: 1500mm × 1500mm clear turning space at landing (BS 8300 / Boverket BFS 2024:12 / Finnish F1).
- Door clear width: ≥900mm (EN 81-70 Type 2; DIN 18040-1; GB 50763; ADA min 915mm).
- Door dwell: ≥6 seconds recommended for users with reduced mobility (EN 81-70).

**Corridor clear widths:**
- Single wheelchair passage: ≥915mm continuously (ADA); ≥900mm (DIN 18040-1/BS 8300 residential); ≥1200mm non-residential.
- Two wheelchair users passing: ≥1525mm (ADA); ≥1800mm (UK Part M non-residential best practice; care settings standard).
- Best practice for primary routes: 1800mm minimum, with 1800×1800mm passing places at maximum 10m intervals where narrowing occurs.
- Power wheelchair users: ≥1500mm for comfortable single passage; ≥1800mm for passing.

**Rest seating intervals:**
- No building code in any of 14 languages searched specifies a maximum seating interval.
- OT clinical evidence (energy conservation, OFS/ME/CFS literature): uninterrupted walking distances >25–30m constitute an exertion threshold for OFS/POTS populations and many ambulant MOB users.
- Best practice: rest seating at ≤25–30m intervals on primary circulation routes; reclining or tilt option for OFS populations (no standard; expert consensus only — THIN-BASE).
- Guidebook position (from GAP-B4-01 resolution): 25–30m range; median 27.5m.

**Automatic doors:**
- Sliding automatic doors eliminate operating force barrier entirely — best practice for primary entrances (BS 8300; DIN 18040-1; ADA; TEK17; DIN/EN all recommend).
- Powered doors with ≥6-second open dwell on primary accessible routes.
- Operating force where manual: ≤22N (BS 8300); ≤30N preferred (OT Tier 1 evidence per MOB BPC Steinfeld et al. 2016); ≤38N maximum (CMHC; ADA guidance).

**Most inclusive provision:** Automatic sliding doors on all primary routes; EN 81-70 Type 2 minimum lift (1100×1400mm) with best practice 1100×1800mm; 1800mm corridor width on primary routes; rest seating at 25–30m intervals with tilt/recline option.
