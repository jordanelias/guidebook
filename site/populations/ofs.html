Orthostatic and Fatigue Spectrum

Evidence confidence: MODERATE (seating, thermal — Co-1/Tier 3); LOW (spatial parameters — zero indexed studies). All provisions carry Tier 5 marker.

Functional Profile

ME/CFS, POTS, MCAS. Defining characteristic: post-exertional malaise (PEM) — symptom worsening disproportionate to exertion, persisting 24+ hours. Prolonged standing is primary trigger for POTS orthostatic intolerance; hot environments worsen all OFS subtypes. MCAS co-occurs with chemical/fragrance sensitivity.
