## Context ‚Äî the two problems are one problem

**Problem 1 (genealogy).** The project's central adjudication ‚Äî best practice (dignity-bearing, most accommodating) vs consensus/precedent ‚Äî currently has no computable form. The corridor-width genealogy proves the stakes: 1500mm turning "circles" in 24 jurisdictions trace to one 1970s anthropometric root (REF-00059/60: "based on research conducted in the 1970s‚Ä¶ need to be updated"); the 1800mm passing consensus has **no traceable root anywhere in the corpus**; while 2440/2400mm has **two independent measurement/participatory roots** (ASL proxemics Co-1; swept-path anthropometry T3 entire-sample) plus an untraced regulatory convergence (MEXT T5 ‚Äî a `committee_assertion` under this DR's own vocabulary until its derivation is mined). Precedent counts documents; evidence counts roots ‚Äî and today nothing in the data layer can count roots. Worse, values are being compared across incommensurable **measurement paradigms** (static turning circle vs dynamic swept-path envelope ‚Äî 97% of turns are asymmetric spin turns; scooters have no pivot at all) and across **device classes** (multiple codes self-declare their figures manual-wheelchair-only).

**Problem 2 (handshake).** The corpus runs two derivation paths with no data-layer reconciliation and only partial, one-directional prose coupling: **top-down** (population/community ‚Äî BPCs, Co-1, every DB join) and **bottom-up** (function ‚Äî the 38-file ICF-indexed `references/fdr/` corpus, deliberately population-blind at collection; the functional-deficit-auditor's population‚ÜîICF mapping, which lives as unvalidated skill prose). No mechanism requires the paths to agree before a value ships; dual derivation is undetectable by query. The two corridor requirements (device envelope, d465; signing envelope, d310+DEAF) sit in the same directory with no common key ‚Äî and DEAF is absent from E-08's `item_population_links` despite holding the anchor evidence, which is precisely the population-silo failure the armature's reverse-mapping (Q4 ‚Äî resolved on paper 2026-04-28, never built) exists to prevent.

They are one problem because both reduce to: **a claim's authority derives from its derivation, and derivations must be recorded, independent-counted, and cross-checked.** Convergence across independent *evidence axes* is already doctrine (#3); this DR extends the identical logic down to roots (genealogy) and up to derivation paths (handshake).

## Proposed decision ‚Äî six separately ratifiable items

### H1 ‚Äî Genealogy fields on `source_value_extractions`

Extend the (empty) B6 substrate with: `root_id` (shared identifier for one evidentiary root), `root_type` CHECK (`measurement_primary` / `participatory_finding` / `committee_assertion` / `derived_calculation` / `untraced`), `root_ref_id` (FK to `evidence_sources` when the root document is in corpus), `echo_of` (transmission-chain note), `measurement_paradigm` CHECK (`swept_path_dynamic` / `static_turning_circle` / `static_clearance` / `anthropometric_percentile` / `instrumented_physical_measurement` ‚Äî acoustic/photometric/force values / `route_metric` ‚Äî distances, intervals / `field_observation` / `participatory_spatial` / `stated_unmeasured`), `device_class` CHECK (`manual_self_propelled` / `manual_attendant` / `power_chair` / `scooter` / `bariatric_manual` / `bariatric_power` ‚Äî the corpus itself distinguishes them (REF-00061; FDR-BAB-04) / `walker_rollator` / `mixed` / `not_device_scoped`), `root_population_note` (the root's sample description ‚Äî the fit-population test), `file_anchor` (path+section into the md/yaml/json corpus ‚Äî the tri-format provenance link). Derived view `v_value_independence`: per (parameter, population), COUNT(DISTINCT root_id) excluding `committee_assertion` ‚Äî **the computable best-practice-vs-consensus discriminator** ‚Äî under three anti-gaming disciplines (adversarial-review hardening): (a) **root registry**: a `root_id` must reference either a `root_ref_id` in `evidence_sources` or a registered external-root stub (one row per out-of-corpus root, e.g. the 1970s VA/HUD studies), so two extractors cannot mint two ids for one root and double the count; (b) **`untraced` rows carry NULL `root_id`** and are excluded from the count ‚Äî untraceability can never out-score a traced chain; (c) **`root_classification_basis` + `contested` fields** per row, so the root-vs-echo judgment is recordably disputable, not free-text folklore.

**Adjudication doctrine (the six tests, applied per extracted value) ‚Äî four encoded, two doctrinal:** Root, Independence, Paradigm-commensurability and Fit-population map directly to the fields above; **Currency** rides the existing supersession machinery (`evidence_sources.superseded_by_ref_id` + DR-2026-05-24 protocol) rather than duplicating it; **Interest** (who convened the consensus?) is recorded in `root_classification_basis` notes ‚Äî doctrinal, not a dedicated column, until a real vocabulary for convening-interest emerges from use. Cross-class and cross-paradigm applications are recorded as *derivations*, never as identities.

### H2 ‚Äî Derivation-path dimension on determinations

Extend the migration-026 `evidence_cell_state` design (0 rows; cheap now, expensive after backfill) with `functional_basis` (mechanism type per FDA ¬ß3 taxonomy + ICF code(s) + supporting refs incl. FDR file anchors) and `derivation_paths` CHECK (`dual` / `population_only` / `function_only`). Rule, in the exact shape of the existing `single_axis` rule: single-path assertion is permitted **only with a named-path rationale**; dual-path convergence is recorded as itself evidence (doctrine #3 extended). **Cultural/dignity-claim protection (hard rule, per Q9 and the CRPD critique):** claims whose normative force is community-rooted remain fully assertable as `population_only` ‚Äî the handshake makes the derivation *explicit*; it never requires a mechanism where the claim is cultural, and it never lets a functional derivation flatten a community claim (no bottom-up override of Co-1). **Boundary criterion (adversarial-review hardening, closing the dodge-the-mechanism loophole without touching the dignity commitment):** "community-rooted" is not self-declared ‚Äî it means *anchored by Co-1/participatory provenance per `co1_source_type`* (dpo_research, advocacy_position, participatory peer-reviewed work) or by an equivalent documented community process. A `population_only` claim without such an anchor is simply a single-path claim owing the standard named-path rationale; it gains no cultural exemption by assertion.

### H3 ‚Äî Materialize the population‚Üîfunction mapping as data

One table (`population_icf_links`): the FDA ¬ß2 mapping + armature Q12 mapping-confidence classifications, promoted from skill prose to provenance-bearing rows (population_code √ó ICF d-code √ó mechanism √ó confidence √ó provenance). **Named reconciliation sub-step required before load (adversarial-review hardening):** the FDA vocabulary and the live `populations` table disagree ‚Äî FDA ¬ß2 uses ABI/ASD/LOW-VISION (none FK-valid; NEU/AUT/VIS are), and ten live codes (ADHD, CFS, DBL, EPI, MCAS, MS, POTS, SENS, ALL, and others) have no FDA row at all. The migration therefore ships with an FDA-code‚Üípopulation-code crosswalk whose every mapping is a recorded, provenance-bearing row, and unmapped codes are logged as findings ‚Äî no silent ASD‚ÜíAUT aliasing, no FK failures discovered at load time. This table is simultaneously the forward map (population ‚Üí characteristic function) and the substrate for the Q4 reverse-mapping algorithm ‚Äî closing, not reopening, the open A3/A7 joint decision. Immediate payoff: reverse-mapping from d310/d465 would have flagged DEAF's absence from E-08's population links mechanically.

### H4 ‚Äî Wire the existing advisory checks into the determination engine as gates

FDA Q4 verdicts UNLINKED/MISLINKED/UNDER-CONSERVATIVE and FDR delta-classification CONTRADICTS force the affected cell to `provisional` (never `stated`) until resolved ‚Äî converting today's advisory gap entries into the agreement mechanism. FDR runs remain commissioned work; the gate binds only where a check has actually run (no silent pretence of coverage). **Deadlock discipline (adversarial-review hardening):** gate rows carry the tier/type of the triggering source (the FDR corpus is Co-2/grey-heavy, and a grey-source CONTRADICTS pinning a T1-anchored cell indefinitely would invert the ladder); resolution is a named path ‚Äî evidence-auditor adjudication recorded on the cell (per the existing FDR CONTRADICTS‚Üíevidence-auditor route), with the adjudication outcome and its rationale stored, so no cell can sit at `provisional` with no owner-of-resolution.

### H5 ‚Äî The harvest pipeline (scattered ‚Üí usable, tri-format)

Codify the flow the owner asked for: **md is where evidence is authored** (BPCs, FDR ‚Äî narrative, citable, human-reviewable); **the DB is where evidence is queryable** (extraction rows, genealogy, determinations, gaps); **yaml/json are interchange only** ‚Äî loaded or retired. Concretely: (a) `data/jurisdictional_values/*.yaml` ‚Üí load path built and verified on pilot copies by the 026 backfill; **canonical load lands at C1**; add an `instrument_status` dimension per unification-DR item 15; (b) the six `references/tier*/co*-verified-sources.json` registries (551 entries) ‚Üí one-time reconciliation audit against `evidence_sources` (disagreements are findings), then redirect-stub retirement per the connection-register precedent ‚Äî **with registry-only metadata migrated first**: `original_tier`/`corrected_tier` history and verification-correction notes land in `evidence_sources` fields, and the registries' alternate ref-id scheme ("01/RAP-‚Ä¶") is recorded as aliases, so retirement loses nothing; (c) FDR markdown ‚Üí the merge the FDR skill promised **was executed for ~12 BPCs as prose duplication and then abandoned** (38 more BPCs carry the section as "Not yet run"; nothing is merged at the data layer) ‚Äî the remaining corpus lands as *extraction rows + `functional_basis` links*, not more prose duplication; (d) gap-filling is engine-driven: computing all cells turns every hole into a queryable `pending` row with category and falsification condition ‚Äî the gap register becomes a work queue, not a memory. Sequence: A2/C1 ‚Üí B6+H1 ‚Üí extraction pass over the md corpus ‚Üí engine backfill ‚Üí registry retirement.
