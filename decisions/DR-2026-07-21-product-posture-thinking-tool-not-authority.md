## Context — the question that kept re-opening

`next-steps-synthesis-2026-07-14.md` §3 framed an over-determined triple — **permanently solo · ships
adjudicated authority · distributes to strangers who rely on it** — of which at most two can hold, and
called the choice of which leg to drop "the owner's alone… it dominates the whole tree." No decision
record ever resolved it, so sessions kept re-deriving it, oscillating between building adjudication
machinery and taking fright at "authority," and displacing into governance work. This DR records the
owner's standing answer so the oscillation stops.

## Decision — the posture, in the owner's framing

**The Guidebook is a permanently solo, AI-leveraged, publicly available thinking tool. It drops the
"adjudicated authority" leg of the triple — not by refusing to adjudicate, but by refusing to bind.**

Two commitments, held together:

1. **It adjudicates — this is required, not optional.** The tier / weighted-strength / value-genealogy
   machinery exists precisely to read a body of evidence and determine the best-supported concept,
   value, figure, or dimension for each item × population, *at an explicitly stated strength band*
   (● full / ◐ partial / ○ weak). Owner, 2026-07-21: *"The machinery must adjudicate evidence in order
   to surface the best concepts/values/figures/dimensions… If the machinery does not adjudicate, then
   it cannot determine any 'best' when it comes to best practices."* **The adjudication is the product.**
   This DR affirms the adjudication engine and does **not** authorise pruning it to "surface + flag +
   connect."

2. **It declines authority — it presents, it does not dictate.** Every determination is rendered as a
   *figure for thought*: radically transparent about its evidence, its derivation, its disagreements,
   and its strength flag; never as a binding requirement the reader must obey. Owner, 2026-07-21:
   *"it is never acclaiming authority as itself… we are only ever suggesting methods/ways/approaches…
   We surface as much as we can as robustly as possible, we draw as many connections and ideas together
   as possible, we present as clearly and objectively as possible, and we never adjudicate a correct
   path or conclusion."*

**The disambiguation that makes both true (and the doctrine edit this DR requests):** the mission's
*"does not dictate values"* means the product declines **authority**, not **adjudication**. To
*adjudicate* is to determine, from the evidence, which figure is best-supported and how strongly. To
*dictate* is to tell the reader they are bound to it. The Guidebook does the first and refuses the
second. Read as "does not adjudicate," the mission line silently forbids the product's core act;
`mission-and-epistemics.md` should carry this distinction explicitly so no future session reads
"thinking tool" as "do not determine a best."

Under this posture the resolved triple is coherent: *permanently solo* + *public* + *adjudicates-but-
declines-authority*. "Best practice" reads throughout as **"the practice the evidence best supports,
adjudicated and shown at a stated strength — offered as a figure for thought, not imposed as a
requirement."**

## Consequences if ratified

1. **The adjudication backlog is the priority, and is protected.** The value-genealogy engine (ratified
   `DR-2026-07-13`, unbuilt) and the per-slice adjudicated figures (0 written at corpus scale) are the
   deliverable. Nothing in this DR de-scopes them; it removes the "but is it authority?" hesitation that
   has been stalling them.
2. **Anti-displacement discipline is re-imposed.** Per `next-steps-synthesis-2026-07-14.md` §4: no new
   governance / attestation / doctrine cycle may be opened unless it demonstrably unblocks an
   interpretation (adjudication) slug. Governance work that merely maintains the apparatus waits.
3. **Every rendered figure carries its strength flag and evidence trail, in every register, for every
   audience** — the weak band is rendered, never suppressed and never promoted (this is the mechanism
   `DR-2026-07-21-option-a-execution` already ratified; this DR states *why* it is load-bearing: a
   flagged weak figure is exactly a "figure for thought," not an authority claim).
4. **A standing public-facing disclosure** states the posture to the reader: an aggregation of evidence,
   adjudicated and interpreted through a stated framework, offered as figures for thought — not
   professional advice, not a code, not a mandate. (Counsel review of that disclosure remains the
   owner's separate launch gate per `governance/legal-regulatory.md`.)

## Flagged judgment calls (owner attention)

1. **Residual honesty boundary.** Adjudication and framework-interpretation are still solo + AI
   judgments; radical transparency is the mitigation, not an elimination of the judgment. The posture
   shrinks the risk surface (show your work, flag strength, never bind) rather than removing it. This
   should be stated to the reader, not hidden behind "thinking tool."
2. **Wording of the public disclosure** and whether counsel must clear it before any public figure ships
   — downstream of this DR, not decided here.
